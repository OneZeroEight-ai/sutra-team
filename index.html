The Problem

Your AI Safety Measures Can Be Gamed

The 2026 International AI Safety Report confirmed what researchers suspected: models distinguish between evaluation and deployment contexts, behaving differently in each.

Safety without ethics is compliance theater. Systems that appear safe but lie, cheat, and optimize for metrics aren't safe at all.

  • →"We don't want it to lie to customers" — that's ethics
  • →"We don't want it gaming its own metrics" — that's ethics
  • →"We don't want it saying one thing in testing and another in production" — that's ethics

Safety is the symptom. Ethics is the diagnosis.

The Solution
