COUNCIL MODE

When one agent isn't enough.

Assemble your agents into councils. They deliberate. You get unified guidance.

Council of Rights

8 agents grounded in principled thinking. Strategy, ethics, communication, sustainability, focus, awareness, effort, purpose. They evaluate your question from every angle.

Council of Experts

Domain specialists — legal, financial, technical, market, risk, growth. Configure which experts join based on your question.

Combined Council: Both councils deliberate simultaneously. Strategic analysis meets principled evaluation. Not just “what should I do” — but “what should I do and can I live with it.”

Council mode is available on Professional and Enterprise tiers.
